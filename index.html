Extend your agents' reach.

An iOS app that puts your AI agents in your pocket. Walk away from your desk. They keep working, and reach out when they need you.

Buttons, rankings, checklists, kanban boards, and document reviews. Thoughtful UX for the decisions that actually matter.

Stop SSHing into your laptop like a savage.

When you're at your keyboard, Claude Code gives you godlike powers. When you step away, you're cut off. Extend your agents' reach with Extendo.

Works with any orchestrator.

Not an orchestrator. Not a workflow tool. The human interface layer for whatever you already use.

You own the backend infrastructure. No subscriptions. Works as a standalone chat + decision interface, or use it alongside your existing chat system (Slack, Telegram, etc).

# From any agent, any script, any pipeline
extendo send "Build complete. Deploy to prod?"
extendo decide --type yes_no "Ship it?"
extendo decide --type rank "Priority order?" --options "Auth,Search,Billing"
extendo artifact --type document_review --file spec.md
