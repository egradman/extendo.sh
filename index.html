Extend your agents' reach.

An iOS app and companion CLI & Skill that puts your AI agents in your pocket. Walk away from your desk. They keep working, and reach out when they need you.

Buttons, rankings, checklists, kanban boards, and document reviews. Thoughtful UX for the decisions that actually matter.

Stop SSHing into your laptop like a savage.

I turned human decision-making into a tool-call because I was tired of using my phone to ssh into my laptop at parties. Structured decisions on your phone from AI agents, voice-first, Apple Watch, open protocol. Any agent can call the CLI and block until the human responds.

One app, all your agents

          
Claude Code, OpenClaw, Slack, custom backends — all your agent threads in one app. One inbox, every agent.

Keep track of open loops

          
Elegant progress dashboards that help you keep track of everything you orchestrate. Even when you bite off more than you can chew.

How it works

      

        

          
1

          
Your agent hits a gate

          
Needs approval, a choice, a review — any point where a human should weigh in.

        

        

          
2

          
Your phone buzzes

          
Rich push notification with structured UI. Tap a button, record a voice note, rank options.

        

        

          
3

          
Your agent keeps going

          
Decision flows back instantly. The agent resumes. You resume talking with your human friends.
